How NuiM Works

Four steps.
Zero filler.

Every project is different. The rigour isn't. Here's how I think through the work — from first call to final export.

01

Dig In

I read the brief three times before I respond. Then I send back better questions. Who is this really for? What would killing it look like in two years? The work gets good here, before anything is drawn.

02

Make a Mess

Paper first. I sketch until I find directions worth showing, then I show them — including the ones that surprise me. Two or three routes, no single answer handed down from on high.

03

Get Obsessive

One direction earns the deep work. I'll adjust a letterform ten times and keep the eleventh. Every pixel has a reason. I can argue for it in plain English if asked.

04

Hand It Over Right

Organised files, a clear brand guide, a walkthrough, and 30 days of follow-up access. You shouldn't need me to use what I made you — but I'm there if you do.
